# CivicFlood AI - Poster

## 🇬🇭 Ghana AI Innovation Challenge 2026

### The Problem
- Ghana floods every rainy season
- 26,000+ displaced by Akosombo spillage (2023)
- Communities receive warnings too late
- Existing forecasts too broad, too technical

### The Solution: CivicFlood AI
An AI-powered community flood intelligence platform that combines satellite data with citizen reports to deliver explainable, localized flood warnings.

### How It Works
1. **Satellite Data** → CHIRPS rainfall + GloFAS river levels
2. **Community Reports** → WhatsApp flood reports
3. **AI Fusion** → XGBoost predicts risk (R²=0.993)
4. **Explainability** → SHAP explains WHY
5. **Impact Estimate** → Population, schools, roads at risk
6. **Alert** → WhatsApp notification with actions

### Key Differentiators
| Feature | CivicFlood AI | Others |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| Explainable | ✅ SHAP | ❌ Black box |
| Community-validated | ✅ WhatsApp | ❌ None |
| Ghana-specific | ✅ Dams, districts | ⚠️ Regional |

### Impact
- **Accuracy:** R² = 0.993
- **Coverage:** 10 Accra districts
- **Lead time:** 24-72 hours
- **Cost:** Zero for Phase 0-1
