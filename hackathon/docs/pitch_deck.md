# CivicFlood AI - Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title
**CivicFlood AI**
AI-Powered Community Flood Intelligence Platform for Ghana
Ghana AI Innovation Challenge 2026

## Slide 2: Problem
- Ghana floods every rainy season
- 26,000+ displaced by Akosombo spillage (2023)
- Communities receive warnings too late or not at all
- Existing forecasts too broad, too technical

## Slide 3: Solution
**CivicFlood AI** combines:
- 🌧️ Satellite intelligence (CHIRPS, GloFAS)
- 📱 Community reports (WhatsApp)
- 🧠 Explainable AI (SHAP)
- 📊 Impact estimation

## Slide 4: How It Works
Satellite Data + Community Reports
         ↓
    AI Prediction (R²=0.993)
         ↓
    Explainable Output
         ↓
    WhatsApp Alert
